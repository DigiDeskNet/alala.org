Florence ANDREWS


ALALA PATRON

Florence Andrews, despite her young years, has great experience as an actress, appearing in many stage productions from the Mikado to the Wizard of Oz. Florence has been an ambassador to the Gesar Foundation since 2011 and has recorded the childrens Anthem Wish Fulfilling Tree when she collaborated with the celebrated composer Paul Englishby. There is no doubting the talent and kind heart of Florence Andrews, we are delighted to have her join us as an ALALA Patron. Thank you Florence for your generosity and enthusiasm to assist young people across the world. ALALA look forward to you entertaining and inspiring many children on all continents.

The Wizard of Oz / Florence

Kelsang PAWO


ALALA AMBASSADOR

Kelsang Pawo is the founder of The Gesar Foundation for children. He has dedicated his life to his belief that all children of the world may be equally cherished and therefore, whatever there background or culture, find the causes for happiness, success and prosperity.

Pawo wrote the lyrics for the Gesar Foundation world Anthem for children Wish Fulfilling Tree that has been recorded by many musicians across the globe. His humanitarian work began in Ethiopia in 2006 where he built a school and formed The Gesar Foundation for the benefit of the world's children. Since that date he has continued to inspire the young to become ambassadors of Gesar and to create their own projects and charities. Several organisations are now active in different parts of the world due to his encouragement.

Jaden CORNELIOUS


ALALA PATRON

Jaden Cornelious was the very first artist to record Wish Fulfilling Tree and has been a Gesar Foundation ambassador since 2009. He formed The Jaden Cornelious Foundation that has been mainly funded by his own singing career. Jaden and his dedicated team have assisted many beings on our planet and now we welcome him as an ALALA patron.
